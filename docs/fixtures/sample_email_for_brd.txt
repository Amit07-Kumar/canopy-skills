Hi team,

We need to build a new flow for verifying premium sellers before they get the "verified" badge on the marketplace. Here is what I have gathered so far from the partnership conversations and the customer success team.

Problem statement:
- Today any seller can get the premium tag by paying — we are not validating their actual fulfilment quality.
- Buyer complaints are up 18% in Q2 because of mismatched expectations.
- Trust score on the marketplace is dropping; competitor B2B platforms are eating into our share in metals and packaging.

What we want to build:
1. Multi-step verification — KYC documents + GST validation + last-12-months fulfilment metrics.
2. Manual review queue for the trust-and-safety team (3-person team in Noida).
3. Re-verification every 6 months automatically.
4. A "Verified Premium" badge with a hover tooltip showing the verification date.
5. API for partner platforms to query verification status.

Users:
- Sellers — submit verification, view status.
- Trust & Safety analysts — review queue, approve/reject.
- Buyers — see badge on listings.
- Partner platforms — read-only API.

Constraints:
- Launch by end of Q3 (September 2026).
- Must integrate with the existing PaymentGateway and Listing services.
- GDPR-equivalent compliance for verification data retention.
- Mobile and web parity.

Open questions:
- Do we charge sellers extra for verification, or include it in premium tier?
- What is the SLA for the manual review queue — 24h or 48h?
- Do we expose verification status via the public seller profile or behind login?

Could you turn this into a proper BRD with functional/non-functional requirements, risk register, and a phased rollout plan?
